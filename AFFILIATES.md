### Commission Models Explained

**Revenue Share (most common):**
- You get 25-40% of net revenue from referred players, lifetime
- Best for long-term income
- Example: Player deposits R10,000, loses R2,000. You get 30% of R2,000 = R600

**CPA (Cost Per Acquisition):**
- One-time payment per qualified player (R500-R2,000)
- Faster payout but no recurring income
- Example: Refer 10 players, get R1,000 each = R10,000 upfront

**Hybrid:**
- Lower CPA + lower revenue share
- Example: R500 upfront + 15% revenue share

### Negotiation Tips

1. **Start with standard terms** - Don't negotiate until you have leverage (traffic)
2. **Track performance** - After 3-6 months, if you're driving good conversions, ask for better rates
3. **Volume matters** - "I'm driving 50+ signups/month, can we discuss 35% instead of 30%?"

### You Must Include:

2. **Responsible Gambling**:
   - 18+ warning
   - Link to National Responsible Gambling Programme: https://responsiblegambling.co.za
   - Helpline: 0800 006 008

3. **License Verification**:
   - Only promote licensed operators
   - Check provincial gambling board licenses

### South African Gambling Regulations

- **Legal:** Yes, online sports betting is legal in SA
- **Regulated by:** Provincial gambling boards (Western Cape, KZN, Mpumalanga, etc.)
- **Tax:** Operators pay tax on turnover; players' winnings are generally tax-free (for recreational betting)
